Constants¶

pMuTT will soon migrate its unit conversions and constants to VUnits!

Constants¶

R

Universal molar gas constant, R

kb

Boltzmann constant

h

Planck’s constant, h

c

Speed of light

Na

Avogadro’s number in molecules/mol

P0

Reference pressure

T0

Room temperature

V0

Molar volume of ideal gas at standard temperature and pressure

e

Electronic charge in Coulombs

m_e

Mass of an electron

m_p

Mass of a proton

Unit Conversions¶

convert_unit

Converts units between two unit sets

debye_to_einstein

Converts Debye temperature to Einstein temperature

einstein_to_debye

Converts Einstein temperature to Debye temperature

energy_to_freq

Converts energy to frequency

energy_to_temp

Converts energy to temperature

energy_to_wavenumber

Converts energy to wavenumber

freq_to_energy

Converts frequency to energy

freq_to_temp

Converts frequency to temperature

freq_to_wavenumber

Converts frequency to wavenumber

inertia_to_temp

Converts moment of inertia into rotational temperature

temp_to_energy

Converts temperature to energy

temp_to_freq

Converts temperature to frequency

temp_to_wavenumber

Converts vibrational/rotational temperature to wavenumber

wavenumber_to_energy

Converts wavenumbers (1/cm) to energies (J)

wavenumber_to_freq

Converts wavenumber to frequency

wavenumber_to_inertia

Converts wavenumber (1/cm) to moment of inertia

wavenumber_to_temp

Converts wavenumbers (1/cm) to temperatures (K)

Misc.¶

atomic_weight

Atomic weight.

prefixes

SI unit prefixes

symmetry_dict

Symmetry number.

type_dict

Key are units, values are the type of unit
