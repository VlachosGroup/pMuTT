pmutt.io.omkm.get_interactions_phases¶

pmutt.io.omkm.get_interactions_phases(interactions, species)

Helper method to organize reaction into its phases

Parameters

  • interactions (list of SurfaceReaction objects) – Lateral interactions to extract phases

  • species (dict of EmpiricalBase objects) – Species corresponding to interactions

Returns

species_phases – Dictionary where the keys are strings of phase names and the values are lists of the reactions

Return type

dict
